Compliance Scan
Hey — just so you know, this is not a normal query. Compliance Scan reads every single document in this collection, top to bottom, no skipping. Nothing gets filtered out.

It's going to be slower, it's going to use more tokens, and it's built for situations where missing something actually matters. If you're just looking something up, regular mode is fine.
