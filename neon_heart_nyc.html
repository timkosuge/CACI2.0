How to use this thing

1 · Put your sign somewhere

Pick a preset from the Location panel, type GPS directly, or enter free-fly mode (spacebar), fly somewhere, and press C to drop the sign where you're looking.

2 · See it from the right angle

Synced mode runs the v9 cinematic automatically — acts 1 through 5 — orbiting the sign. Free fly (press Space) lets you fly anywhere while the sign stays locked at its GPS anchor.

3 · Make it perfect

Scrub the timeline. P pauses. Tap , and . for frame-by-frame. Orient with heading/pitch/roll sliders.

4 · Record it

Click ⏺ RECORD, pick a quality, pick a folder. A 38-second cycle renders as a frame sequence.
